Staff Meeting on the 20th!

Jan 17th 2024 By Brokk Announcement Updated on: Jan 17th 2024

Hello mappers! We are starting 2024 with a team meet-up discussing our plans for the year. There are various projects coming up, some public, some not. You may have already seen Nesman’s improvements to our map queue (bot.tf2maps.net), which has been completely rewritten, as well as the discussions a few weeks ago about the Featured Maps event. There is a lot to talk about, but if there is anything that is especially close to your heart, let us know so we can add it to the agenda. We can’t promise we will talk about everything, but we will at the very least consider it. Cheers!

Brokk
